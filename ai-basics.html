🤖 AI Basics

Artificial Intelligence is the ability of computers and machines to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence.

What Is Machine Learning?

Machine learning is a branch of AI that allows computers to learn from data and improve their performance without being programmed for every single task.

What Are Algorithms?

Algorithms are step-by-step instructions that computers follow to solve problems, process information, and make decisions.

Why Is Data Important?

AI systems learn by studying data. The quality and accuracy of the data strongly affect how well an AI system performs.

Examples of AI Around Us

Voice assistants, recommendation systems, navigation apps, image recognition, chatbots, and fraud detection all use AI.
