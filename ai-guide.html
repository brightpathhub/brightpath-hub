What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to computer systems that can perform tasks that normally require human intelligence. These tasks include learning, solving problems, recognizing images, understanding language, and making decisions.

Today, AI is used in smartphones, online search engines, healthcare, banking, education, transportation, and many other areas of everyday life.

As AI continues to evolve, learning about it can help people develop valuable skills and better understand the technology shaping the future.
