Resume Writing Guide

Create a clear, professional resume that presents your skills and experience effectively.

1. Contact Information

Include your full name, phone number, professional email address, city, country, and LinkedIn profile when relevant.

2. Professional Summary

Write two or three sentences describing your experience, strongest skills, and the value you can provide to an employer.

3. Work Experience

List your most recent job first. Include your job title, employer, dates of employment, responsibilities, and measurable achievements.

4. Education

Add your degree, diploma, institution, location, and year of completion. Include relevant certifications where appropriate.

5. Skills

Mention technical skills and workplace skills that match the job description. Avoid adding skills that are unrelated to the role.

6. Keep It Clear

Use short sentences, simple formatting, consistent headings, and bullet points. One or two pages is usually enough.

Common Resume Mistakes

Avoid spelling errors, unnecessary personal information, very long paragraphs, dishonest claims, and using the same resume for every job.

Resume Checklist

  • Contact details are correct
  • Resume is tailored to the job
  • Important skills are easy to find
  • Achievements are included
  • Spelling and grammar are checked
  • Formatting is consistent
